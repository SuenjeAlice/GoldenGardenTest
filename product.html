Landscaping Product

Donegal Quartz

This is a medium sized stone larger than the 20mm but smaller than the chunky stone. It is vibrant in colour and brightens up any garden.

10mm Beech Pebble

A special mixture of colours, it works well in smaller areas. It is great for percolation and drainage of yards, removes any locks of water.

20mm Beech Pebble

This is a slightly larger version of the 14mm is also a beautiful stone. This is beautiful on display in a garden. It is also mulit coloured with a range of colours you never tire of. It is a fantastic stone to lay where percolation is an issue.

Charcoal Grey

Charcoal Grey is the perfect stone to get a dark finish to your yard. The colour works well around houses and is easy to maintain.

Pale Brown

Pale Brown is a lovely stone, easy on the eye and with delicate colour gives a refreshing finish.

Crushed Plum Slate

This plum slate has been very popular for the past few years. Rich in colour and gives a lovely finish in garden and flower beds.

Leitrim Gold

This stone is from the heart of Leitrim. It is a classy light golden colour. If you want to brighten up an area, this is the stone that you never tire of looking at.

Cranford Gold

Cranford is new to our range for this year. Cranford is a lovely rich colour giving a classy finish when laid.

Alpine White

Mixing Alpine White with Charcoal Grey makes it it stand out more and is usually used on garden beds.

Small Water Features

One of our large round stone for rockeries and water features. This stone is very popular because it is small enough to handle and gives a lovely finisb on slopes and water features.

Large Boulders

Popular in larger water features. For people landscaping a stream, it also protects the side of a stream from erosion.

5mm Beech Pebble

This is our smallest stone, ideal in flower boxes and small confined spaces, rich in a mixture of colours. It is also used as potting grit to help percolation in flower pots.
